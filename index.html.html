Project Workflow 5 STAGES
01

Data Collection

Water quality dataset collected from Kaggle containing 1000 real-world water samples with 11 chemical and physical parameters — pH, Dissolved Oxygen, Turbidity, Electrical Conductivity, TDS, Nitrate, Phosphate, BOD, COD, Coliform Bacteria and Ammonia.

02

Data Preprocessing

Raw data cleaned and a balanced Water Quality Score computed using weighted formula: DO(30%) + BOD(20%) + COD(20%) + Ammonia(20%) + base(10%). Dynamic threshold of 0.5792 applied. Dataset: 509 Good and 491 Bad samples.

03

Model Training

Two Random Forest models trained — Classifier predicts Good/Bad category, Regressor predicts Quality Score. 120 trees, 80/20 split — 800 training and 200 testing samples.

04

Prediction & Classification

User enters 11 parameters manually. Model predicts Water Quality Score and classifies as Good Water ✅ or Bad Water ⚠️. Model achieved 95% accuracy on test data.

05

Pollution Source Identification

When water is Bad — BOD, COD and Coliform are checked. High COD → Industrial Pollution. High BOD or Coliform → Domestic Sewage. Risk Level calculated. Solutions recommended.
